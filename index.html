About

Based in Toronto, MERS is quickly emerging as a compelling new voice in the global electronic music landscape. She brings an intoxicating sound, confident selection, and an unshakeable sense of style. MERS delivers fast, hard-hitting techno characterized by searing acid basslines and punchy grooves — a masterclass in controlled aggression, a vibrant interplay of force and playfulness. Each set is a relentless exploration of the raw, emotional power of the dancefloor.
